Preferred Name: Serg (he/him)
Program: MPH in Population and Family Health

From: Pastoria City, Sinnoh
Starter Pokémon: Chimchar

Background: Grew up near the Great Marsh, spending childhood studying the behaviors of local wild Pokémon and leveraging data science techniques to gain a combat advantage in Pokémon battles. By putting his Pokémon data science nerd on, he developed a talent for reading opponents’ strategies in battle and a love for statistical modeling. Based on this Pokéstats work, he spends most of his time using data to build perfect individual values (IVs) in key stats for the purpose of competitive play.

Overall Goal: He wants to become a field researcher to document the diverse Pokémon ecosystem across the Sinnoh region and to uncover the secrets of the region’s legendary Pokémon, including Darkrai, Regigigas, and Heatran.

Contributions: Project Design, Data Sourcing/Identification, Data Cleaning, Hypothesis Testing, Regression Modeling, Data Interpretation, Manuscript/Final Report Development.

Preferred Name: Spence (she/her)
Program: MPH in Epidemiology
Certificate: Applied Biostatistics and Public Health Data Science
From: Castelia City, Unova
Starter Pokémon: Oshawott

Background:. Raised amidst Castelia’s bustling harbor, Spence grew up charting currents, cataloging marine Pokémon behavior, and learning how environments shape health. She blends epidemiologic methods with biostatistics and public health data science to study transmission dynamics, whether that’s a pathogen in a port city or status effects spreading through a battle. Her field notes with Oshawott evolved into full-fledged dashboards tracking encounter rates, type distributions, and move efficacy. With a soft spot for aquatic ecosystems, she’s especially interested in how water-quality metrics and coastal infrastructure impact both human communities and Water-type habitats.

Training Style: Prefers control and resilience over brute force. Spence uses hazard management (Recover, Wish, and screens), type resistances, and status mitigation to outlast opponents while setting up decisive win conditions. She leans on Water-types like Vaporeon, Jellicent, Mega Starmie, and Spheal for bulk, utility, and reliable coverage, then surprises foes with Cinccino’s multi-hit pressure to break through stalls. Careful team synergy, pivoting, and data-informed move selection help her counter meta threats and turn small advantages into checkmates.

Overall Goal: To become a field epidemiologist who maps health risks across coastal and riverine regions, integrating Pokémon ecology with public health surveillance. She aims to build open, data-driven tools that protect communities and conserve Water-type habitats.

Contributions: Project Design, Site Design/Development, Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Data Interpretation.
